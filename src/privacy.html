Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 March 2026 · Traxent Ltd, registered in England & Wales

Traxent is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. We do not sell your data to any third party.

Who we are

Traxent is operated by Traxent Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales. Our platform is an educational trading tool — we do not provide financial advice, manage investments, or hold client funds..

What data we collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the service:

  • Email address — used to create your account and send service communications
  • Name — used to personalise your dashboard experience
  • Subscription and billing data — processed securely by Stripe on our behalf
  • Simulated trade journal entries — data you voluntarily log within the platform
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, session duration (anonymised)
  • Technical data — browser type, device type, IP address (for security purposes)

We do not collect any real trading account data, brokerage credentials, or financial account information.

How we use your data

  • To create and manage your Traxent account
  • To process your subscription payments via Stripe
  • To calculate and display your prop firm readiness scores
  • To send transactional emails such as receipts and account notifications
  • To improve the platform based on aggregated usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

We will never use your data to make automated decisions that have a legal or significant effect on you.

Third party services

We use the following third party services to operate Traxent:

  • Auth0 — user authentication and account management
  • Stripe — subscription billing and payment processing
  • Amazon Web Services — cloud infrastructure and data storage
  • Formspree — waitlist email capture

Each of these providers operates under their own privacy policies and is subject to appropriate data processing agreements. Your payment card details are never stored by Traxent — they are handled entirely by Stripe.

Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription and request account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or tax purposes.

Anonymised, aggregated usage data may be retained indefinitely as it cannot be used to identify you.

Your rights under UK GDPR

As a UK resident you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at data.gdpr@traxent.io. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

Traxent uses essential cookies only — these are required for authentication and to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising cookies or third party tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings but this will prevent you from logging in to the platform.

Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data is transmitted over HTTPS, passwords are never stored in plain text, and access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel only. Our infrastructure is hosted on Amazon Web Services in the United Kingdom.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by displaying a notice on the platform. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at datapolicy@traxent.io.
