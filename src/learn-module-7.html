Choosing & connecting your trading account

You've done the work and your readiness score says you're prepared. This module is the bridge from practising in Traxent to trading for real — choosing a platform, practising on it in demo, connecting it to Traxent so your real performance scores itself, and stepping into an evaluation with live data behind you.

This onboarding is designed for traders who have reached 85%+ readiness on a tracked firm. Not there yet? Keep logging trades on the readiness tracker first.
Why connect an account at all? Up to now your score has used your sim journal. The moment Traxent reads your real fills, the score becomes ungameable and genuinely trustworthy — it runs each firm's exact drawdown maths (trailing vs end-of-day vs static) on what you actually did, and can even tell you whether your last 90 days would have passed the challenge you're about to buy.

The four steps

1

Confirm your best-fit firm

Open the readiness tracker and check the best-matched firm panel. It ranks at least 16 firms against how you actually trade — your drawdown discipline, consistency and trading days — and names the one whose rules fit you best.

Open best-match →
2

Choose a platform you can practise on — and that Traxent syncs with

Pick a trading platform that (a) matches your firm's asset class and (b) Traxent can read, so your trades flow in automatically. Start in demo / paper mode — it behaves like the real thing but risks nothing, and it's the cleanest way to rehearse the exact firm rules before you pay an evaluation fee.

  • Futures (Apex, Topstep, MyFundedFutures, Tradeify…): Tradovate, NinjaTrader or Rithmic.
  • Forex / indices (FTMO, FundedNext, FXIFY…): cTrader or MetaTrader 4/5.
See supported platforms →
3

Connect it to Traxent (read-only)

On the Integrations page, connect your account. Traxent only ever reads your closed trades and equity curve to score them — it never places trades or touches your funds. Your real performance then updates your readiness score and journal automatically.

Connect an account →
4

Attempt the evaluation — then track the funded phase

When your real, connected data holds above the firm's rules, buy the evaluation with confidence. After you pass, keep the account connected: Traxent tracks payout readiness and drawdown discipline on funded capital, so staying funded becomes as measurable as getting funded.
