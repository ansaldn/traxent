# Traxent — TikTok & Instagram Marketing Plan

## The positioning (this is the whole edge)
The prop-trading space is drowning in hype: Lamborghinis, "I made $10k today," fake gurus. Traxent
is the **honest, evidence-based** alternative — *"know when you're actually ready before you pay for
a challenge."* Every piece of content should reinforce: **data over hype, losses are normal,
readiness is earned.** That voice is rare in this niche, it's defensible, and it's exactly why an
honest influencer strategy (showing losses, not just wins) fits the brand.

One-line promise for bios: **"Stop paying for prop challenges you're not ready for."**

## Why short-form, why now
- FinTok/FinReels is an education-first community — people come to *learn*, not be sold to. That's
  our lane. (Source: Sprout Social FinTok.)
- The For You Page is a level playing field: a 0-follower account can hit millions if the content
  resonates — you don't need an audience to start. (Source: TikTok 2026 playbooks.)
- Reels get ~2.5× the reach of photos; the algorithm weights **saves and shares** over likes, and
  the **first 3 seconds** decide everything. Build for save-ability (useful) and a hard hook.
- Users search TikTok/IG like Google — titles should be the **questions traders type**
  ("how does FTMO drawdown work", "why do I keep failing prop challenges").

## Content pillars (rotate these)
1. **Firm rules, decoded** — one firm/rule per video. "FTMO's 5% daily loss limit, explained in
   20 seconds." You have 16 firms × ~5 rules = 80+ videos before repeating. High search value.
2. **Why traders fail** — the 80–90% fail rate and the *behavioural* reasons (tilt, revenge,
   over-sizing). Myth-busting, relatable, shareable. Pull straight from learn Modules 5 & 6.
3. **Readiness / the product** — screen-record the tracker: "I'm 88% ready for Apex — here's the
   one rule holding me back." Shows the tool without selling it.
4. **Honest reality** — normalise losing days, show a red day, "what a disciplined loss looks
   like." This is the anti-guru content that builds trust (and seeds the influencer angle).
5. **Comparisons** — "Topstep vs Apex in 30s," "cheapest firm to get funded," "which firm has no
   daily loss limit." Straight from `/compare`. Strong search + decision-stage intent.
6. **Psychology / mindset** — discipline, FOMO, the recovery-trade trap. Evergreen, saveable.

## Formats that work for this niche
- **Talking head + b-roll of charts** (build a face for the brand — trust).
- **Screen-recording the tracker/compare page** with a voiceover (product-led, no hard sell).
- **Text-overlay infographics** (the assets in `marketing/assets/` — post as carousels/Reels).
- **Green-screen "reacting" to a firm's rules page** or to a fake-guru clip (the honest foil).
- **Carousels on IG** for "the 5 rules that fail most traders" — high save rate.

## Hooks (first 3 seconds — steal these)
- "You're not ready for a prop challenge. Here's how to know for sure."
- "90% of traders fail FTMO. It's almost never their strategy."
- "Stop paying £500 for challenges you keep failing."
- "The daily loss limit isn't what you think it is."
- "I ranked all 16 prop firms by how hard they are to pass."
- "This is what a disciplined losing day looks like."

## Cadence (sustainable beats heroic)
- **TikTok:** 1 video/day (or 5/week). Volume + the FYP = compounding reach.
- **Instagram Reels:** repurpose the same videos, 4–5/week. Add 1–2 carousels/week (saves).
- **Stories (IG):** daily, casual — polls ("which firm are you on?"), behind-the-scenes, reposts.
- Batch-film weekly (8–10 videos in one session) so daily posting isn't daily filming.

## Hashtags / discovery
Mix broad + niche, ~4–6 per post: `#proptrading #fundedtrader #ftmo #apextraderfunding
#daytrading #futurestrading #forex #fintok #tradingpsychology #propfirm`. More important than
hashtags: put the **search keyword in the on-screen text, caption, and spoken first line**.

## Funnel & CTA
Awareness (FYP video) → Profile (clear bio + "Stop paying for challenges you're not ready for")
→ **Link in bio → /waitlist** (or free Module 1) → email nurture → paid plan. Keep CTAs soft on
TikTok ("link in bio if you want the free readiness check"); the content earns the click.

## Measurement (weekly)
Leading: views, **watch-through %**, **saves + shares** (the real signal), profile visits,
follower growth. Lagging: link clicks → waitlist signups → trials → paid. Track which **pillar**
and **hook** drives saves/clicks and double down. Tag bio link with UTMs.

## Compliance (do not skip — trading content gets restricted)
- Every video: educational, **not financial advice**; "all activity is simulated"; "not FCA
  regulated." A pinned comment / on-screen disclaimer covers most of it.
- Avoid performance/income claims and anything that looks like a financial promotion (UK FCA rules
  on financial promotions are strict; platforms also restrict financial-services ads). Lead with
  *education and readiness*, never "make money."
- For paid influencer posts, require **#ad/#sponsored** disclosure.

## First 30 days (focus)
1. Lock the bio + link (/waitlist), brand colours, and a simple visual template.
2. Batch-film 20 videos across pillars 1, 2, 5 (highest search intent).
3. Post daily on TikTok, repurpose to Reels. Reply to every comment (algorithm + trust).
4. Soft-launch the influencer outreach (see `INFLUENCERS.md`) once you have 10–15 posts live so
   creators see an established feed.
