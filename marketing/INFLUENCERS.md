# Traxent — Influencer / Educator Plan

You asked for honest educators — people who show **losses as well as wins** — to put on the
platform for a real, unscripted review. That's the right instinct: in a niche full of fake gurus,
a credible "I tested it and here's the honest truth" review is worth more than ten paid hype posts.
This doc is a **framework + vetting checklist + a starting shortlist to research**, deliberately
not a "just sign these people" list — the space changes fast and authenticity must be verified.

## What "good" looks like (vet against this)
Prioritise creators who:
- **Show real losing days / drawdowns**, not just wins. Search their feed for red days.
- Have a **verifiable funded track record** (payout proof, but realistic — not "$50k a week").
- **Teach** (process, rules, psychology) rather than sell signals or courses-as-MLM.
- Have **engaged** audiences (comments that are real questions), not just follower counts. Check
  engagement rate, comment quality, and for bot/giveaway-farmed followings.
- Are **compliant-friendly**: disclose #ad, avoid income guarantees, no "get rich" framing.
- **Brand fit**: their audience is aspiring funded traders (futures/forex), UK/global, who'd
  actually use a readiness tool.

Red flags: rented Lambos, "100% win rate," screenshots with no account context, audiences full of
emoji-spam comments, courses sold via urgency/scarcity, no losing content ever.

## Tier strategy (where the ROI is)
- **Micro (10k–100k)** — best ROI. A documented case study showed a single micro-influencer's
  "How I Got Funded" series drove a **250% increase in trader applications** for a prop firm. Start
  here: 5–8 micro creators > 1 big name.
- **Mid (100k–500k)** — one or two for reach once the micro tests prove the message.
- **Macro (500k+)** — later, for credibility/awareness, only after you know which message converts.

## The offer (built for honesty)
Don't pay for a scripted ad. **Give them free Funded Trader access and ask for an honest review —
including what they'd change.** Structure:
1. Free lifetime/12-month Funded Trader account.
2. A "test it for 2 weeks, post your real readiness journey" brief — explicitly invite criticism.
3. Optional: an affiliate/referral code (revenue share) so it's ongoing, not one-and-done.
4. Optional paid usage rights to repost their video as an ad (with #ad).
This is cheaper, more credible, and the content is reusable.

## Discovery — where to build your list
- **Feedspot Top-100 Trading TikTok / Prop-Trading YouTube** lists (ranked, with metrics) —
  the fastest way to a vetted longlist.
- TikTok/IG **search**: "funded account journey," "prop firm review," "FTMO honest," "failed prop
  challenge" — the creators making *honest* content surface here, not under "trading millionaire."
- **Modash / similar** influencer databases for engagement-rate and audience-authenticity checks.
- Your own audience once you launch — the most authentic reviewers are real users.

## Outreach (keep it short, lead with the honesty angle)
> Hi [name] — we built Traxent because the prop space is full of hype and we wanted the opposite:
> a tool that tells traders, with data, whether they're actually ready for a challenge *before*
> they pay. Your content stands out because you show the real ups and downs. We'd love to give you
> free Funded Trader access, no script — test it and tell your audience the honest truth, good or
> bad. If it's useful, we can set up a referral deal. Worth a quick call?

## Process
1. Build a 30-name longlist (Feedspot + searches).
2. Score each against the checklist (losses shown? real track record? engagement? fit?). Keep top ~10.
3. Reach out to 6–8 micro creators with the offer above.
4. Give access, let them post honestly, **measure** referral signups per creator (unique codes).
5. Double down on whoever drives real signups; formalise an affiliate program.
