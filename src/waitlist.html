What is Traxent

Track your path to funded

Traxent is an education-first platform for traders aiming to pass a prop firm challenge. Learn the patterns, log your sim trades, and let the readiness tracker tell you exactly which firm you're prepared for — before you pay a penny for a real challenge.

📖
Learn the craft
Four structured modules cover the candlestick patterns, market structure, risk discipline, and prop-firm-specific quirks you actually need to know.
📓
Sim trade, log, measure
Practice with simulated trades. Every entry is logged, every rule violation is flagged, and your win-rate plus R:R metric build the picture of a real funded-ready trader.
🎯
Score against real rules
16 prop firms — FTMO, Apex, Topstep, MyFundedFutures, FundedNext and more — each firm's published rules are baked into the scoring engine. You'll see which one you're ready for before you spend money on a challenge.
● TestFlight beta · iPhone

Traxent, now in your pocket

The native iOS app brings the readiness tracker, learning modules, position calculator and your sim journal to iPhone — signed in with the same account, so your progress and firm selections sync straight across from the web. Built to feel at home on iOS: full VoiceOver and Dynamic Type support, and light or dark to match your device.

Who can test it: members on the Challenger and Funded Trader plans. Once you're on an eligible plan, your TestFlight invite goes out by email — no separate sign-up needed.
